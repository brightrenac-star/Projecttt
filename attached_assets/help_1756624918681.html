Regional Legal & Compliance

Choose your country/region (draft content, not legal advice).

United States

  • DMCA safe harbor: Designated Agent + clear Notice/Counter-Notice.
  • 18 U.S.C. ¬ß2257 (for explicit content): age-verification records & custodian notice.
  • COPPA: no targeting under 13s.
  • CCPA/CPRA: privacy rights & DSAR workflow.
  • OFAC/AML: if custodial/treasury‚ÄîKYC/screening as needed.
  • Taxes: sales/marketplace facilitator rules.

EU / EEA

  • GDPR: lawful basis, DPA/SCCs, data subject rights, DPO (if required).
  • DSA: notice & action, transparency reporting, EU point of contact.
  • VAT (OSS): cross-border digital services.
  • Consumer: withdrawal rights (exceptions for started digital delivery).

United Kingdom

  • UK GDPR & DPA.
  • Online Safety Act 2023.
  • VAT for digital services.

Thailand

  • PDPA: notice, rights, DPO (if applicable), cross-border transfer.
  • Computer Crime Act: lawful takedown process.
  • VAT e-service (if applicable).
  • 18+: age-gating for NSFW.

Singapore

  • PDPA (SG): collection/use/disclosure rules.
  • IMDA codes: content restrictions.
  • PSA: DPT service providers (if applicable).

Japan

  • APPI: purpose notice, cross-border transfers.
  • Provider Liability Act: defamation/infringement procedures.
  • JCT: consumption tax on digital services.

South Korea

  • PIPA/ICNA: privacy & security obligations.
  • Youth protection: NSFW age controls.

Brazil

  • LGPD: controller/processor duties & rights.
  • Marco Civil: provider liability framework.

Australia

  • Online Safety Act: harmful content responses.
  • GST: digital services.

Canada

  • PIPEDA (+ provincial laws): privacy rules.
  • GST/HST: digital services.

India

  • IT Rules 2021: grievance & takedown timelines.
  • GST: digital services.
  • Age-gating for NSFW.
